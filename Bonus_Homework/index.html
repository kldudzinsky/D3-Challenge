Age and Income:

Above we have a scatter plot depicting the relation between Income and Age in the United States.

There are three distinct clusters and a few outliers. The first one, on the left, has a wider range of income has income ranging between 40,000-50,000 with ages ranging from 43 to 46. The second, middle one, has shows an income ranging between 57,000-63,000, with age ranging from 33-40. The third, far right, shows income ranging from 68,000-74,000 with an age range of 32-40.

We can conclude from the scatter plot, that most people between the age of 36-41 in the United States have an income ranging between 43,000 and 53,000.
